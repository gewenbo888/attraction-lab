# Attraction Lab · 吸引力实验室

Attraction Lab analyses the seven vectors of human magnetism — charisma, emotional energy, voice rhythm, eye contact, mystery, dominance, flirt style — and runs them through three interactive labs and an honest aura reader. Bilingual (EN / 中文), dark/light, custom psychological brand (Bodoni Moda italic display + Outfit body + JetBrains Mono on warm-deep `#0a0814` with magnetic-amber primary `#ffb066` plus rose, voice-cyan, mystery-violet, gold, crimson, emerald accent codes; Noto Serif SC for Chinese display). Hero centerpiece: animated SVG aura field with seven coloured satellite nodes orbiting a glowing core. Eight modules: A1 Seven Vectors of Magnetism (eight cards including Presence as the composite), A2 Aura Wheel (interactive 7-axis radar with live sliders), A3 Voice Rhythm Lab (4 sliders → 6 band classifications: compressed-fast, monotone-slow, over-projected, magnetic-low, magnetic-mid, neutral), A4 Eye-Contact Curve (3 sliders → 6 bands: avoidant, predatory, over-locked, magnetic-band, flickering, neutral), A5 Flirt Style Taxonomy (6 styles × description × cost line, adapted from Hall et al. 2010), A6 Mystery Factor (3 cards: mystery vs withholding, asymmetric information cheap, mystery half-life), A7 Aura Reader (5 multi-paragraph canned probes — charisma deficit, voice-monotone fix, eye-contact overcorrection, mystery without coldness, dominance without arrogance — plus free-text heuristic fallback that detects 8 vector vocabularies plus invisibility/over-projection signals), A8 Guardrails (explicit refusal of pickup-artist scripts, looksism rating, deterministic typology, "read this person to manipulate them" framing, and infinite-AI-generation slop).

## Stack

Plain HTML, CSS, vanilla JavaScript. No build step. Bilingual via `[data-lang]` + CSS attribute selectors. Light/dark via `[data-theme]`. Bodoni Moda italic display, Outfit body, JetBrains Mono mono, Noto Serif SC for Chinese display. All visualisations inline SVG. JS verified with `node --check`.
